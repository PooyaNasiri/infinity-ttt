## üéÆ How to Play

* **Objective**: Align 3 symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.
* **The Twist**: You only have 3 "lives" (pieces).
* **The Warning**: On your turn, if you have 3 pieces on the board, your oldest piece will start to fade.
* **The Shift**: Placing your next piece will delete the fading one. Plan your moves two steps ahead!
